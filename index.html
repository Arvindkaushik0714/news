Earthquake Tremors Felt In Delhi, Neighbouring Areas; Second Time This Week

New Delhi: Tremors were felt in Delhi and adjoining states around 8 pm today, prompting many to rush out of their houses and offices, in the second such earthquake felt in the region. The epicentre was again in Nepal, while the magnitude was 5.4, reported the National Center for Seismology.
Nepal's earthquake monitoring authority said the epicentre was around Patadewal in Bajhang district and the exact time was 8.12 pm local time, meaning 7.57 pm in India. "This is the fifth earthquake in Nepal in the last three days with an intensity of 4 or above," the authority said.
